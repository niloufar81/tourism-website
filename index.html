Discover Khorramabad!

Khorramabad, the capital of Lorestan Province, is known for its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural landscapes.
The city is surrounded by beautiful mountains and features a wealth of historical sites that reflect the history and traditions of the region.
Visitors can explore ancient castles, lush valleys, and picturesque gardens and join the adventure!
